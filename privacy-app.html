Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Terremoto is designed to provide earthquake information without collecting personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, rent, share, or track any personally identifiable information. The app does not require account creation and does not use third‑party analytics SDKs for profiling.

Data Sources

Earthquake event data is retrieved from authoritative public geological data services. These data sets include event identifiers, magnitude, depth, coordinates, intensity indicators, island classification, and timestamp. This information is purely informational and not tied to a user profile.

Device & Network

The app performs standard network requests to fetch updated earthquake information. No custom telemetry is transmitted beyond necessary API calls.

Third-Party Services

The app may display map tiles and related geospatial data. These services may receive anonymized, routine network information (IP address, general device type) needed to deliver map content. We do not augment or forward this data.

Children's Privacy

Terremoto does not target children specifically and does not knowingly collect data from users of any age.

Your Consent

By using the app you consent to this privacy policy. If policies change, we will update this page and adjust the app description where appropriate.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please open an issue on our public repository or contact us through the App Store listing.
