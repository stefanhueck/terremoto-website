1. Privacy at a Glance

General Information

The following notes provide a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit this website. Personal data is any data that can be used to identify you personally. Detailed information on the subject of data protection can be found in the full privacy policy below.

Data Collection on This Website

Who is responsible for data collection on this website?
The data processing on this website is carried out by the website operator (see contact below).

How do we collect your data?

Your data may be collected when you provide it directly (for example via a contact form). Other data is collected automatically when you visit the website using our IT systems — primarily technical data such as browser type, operating system, or time of access.

What do we use your data for?

Some data is required to operate the website correctly, other data may be used to analyze user behaviour or to process contact requests.
